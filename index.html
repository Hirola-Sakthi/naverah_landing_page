Overview

Brigade Insignia

At Brigade Insignia, your home becomes a reflection of your personal style. Spread across 6 acres of pristine land, this exclusive enclave of thoughtfully designed 3 and 4-bedroom residences offers abundant light, space, and ventilation.

Located along the airport highway with seamless metro connectivity, Brigade Insignia keeps you effortlessly connected to the heart of the city while preserving a calm, green sanctuary. The project enjoys close proximity to some of Bengaluru’s key landmarks, including one of Asia’s largest malls, Manyata Tech Park, Manipal Hospital, Outer Ring Road (ORR), Yelahanka New Town, and Kempegowda International Airport (KIA).

Inspired by its surroundings, Brigade Insignia features elegant residences complemented by a refined rooftop retreat, ideal for intimate gatherings. Here, light and air flow freely throughout the day, while evenings unfold into serene, star-lit moments.

About Developer

Brigade Group is one of India’s leading real estate developers, with over three and a half decades of experience in creating enduring value for all stakeholders. Founded in 1986, the company has delivered several landmark developments that have reshaped the skylines of Bengaluru, Chennai, and Kochi, and has also expanded its presence to GIFT City, Gandhinagar, Gujarat. Brigade’s portfolio spans Residential, Commercial, Retail, Hospitality, and Education sectors.

Since its inception, Brigade Group has completed over 275 projects, comprising more than 80 million sq. ft. of developed space across a diverse real estate portfolio. The company is known for its best-in-class design philosophy and world-class amenities that reflect elegance, quality, and sophistication.

Brigade’s residential offerings include villas, villaments, penthouses, premium residences, luxury apartments, value homes, urban studios, senior living communities, plotted developments, and integrated mixed-use lifestyle enclaves and townships. Over the years, Brigade has delivered several pioneering developments, such as Brigade Gateway—Bengaluru’s first lifestyle enclave—and Brigade Exotica, one of the city’s tallest residential landmarks.
